Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working with us.

How long does a website project take?

Most brochure websites take 4–6 weeks from kickoff to launch. Larger projects such as SaaS platforms or WooCommerce stores typically run 8–12 weeks depending on scope. We agree a timeline before work begins and keep you updated at every stage.

Do you work with businesses outside the UK?

Yes. While we are based in Kent, we work with clients across the UK and internationally. Video calls, shared project boards, and clear communication mean distance is never a barrier.

What's included in your WordPress solutions?

Our WordPress service covers custom theme development, plugin development, WooCommerce stores, migration from other platforms, and security hardening. Every build includes responsive design, SEO foundations, performance optimisation, and a content management walkthrough so you can update your site with confidence.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

Absolutely. We offer managed hosting, regular updates, security monitoring, and priority support packages. Many of our clients have been with us for years because we treat their sites as our own.

How much does a website cost?

Every project is different, so we quote based on your specific requirements. A simple brochure site starts from around £2,000, while more complex builds are scoped individually. Get in touch for a free, no-obligation quote.
