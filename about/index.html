About Strength 9

Who we are, what drives us, and why we do things differently.

Built from the Ground Up

Strength 9 Design started as a solo developer with a simple belief: the web deserves better. What began as one person writing clean code and crafting striking designs has grown into a full-service design agency that clients trust to deliver real results.

We are passionate about every pixel and every line of code. Quality is not a buzzword here — it is the standard we hold ourselves to on every project. We would rather take on fewer projects and do them brilliantly than spread ourselves thin.

Based in the UK and working with clients worldwide, we bring a global perspective to every brief. From startups finding their voice to established businesses ready for a digital refresh, we build websites and products that perform, convert, and last.

Our focus has always been quality over quantity. Every project gets our full attention, our best thinking, and the kind of care that only comes from a team that genuinely loves what they do.

How We Work

Four principles that guide every project, every decision, and every line of code we write.

Quality First

We never cut corners. Every website we build is hand-crafted with clean, semantic code, tested across devices, and optimised for performance. If it is not up to our standard, it does not ship.

Transparent Process

No jargon, no black boxes. We keep you in the loop at every stage — from initial concept through to launch. You will always know what we are working on, why, and what comes next.

Performance Obsessed

Speed is not optional — it is essential. We obsess over load times, Core Web Vitals, and Lighthouse scores because a fast site means better user experience, better SEO, and better conversions.

Long-term Partners

We do not just build websites and walk away. We build relationships. Many of our clients have been with us for years because we treat their business like our own and are always here when they need us.
